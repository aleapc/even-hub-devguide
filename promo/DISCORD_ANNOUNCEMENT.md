# Even Realities Discord — Community Announcement

For posting in the Even Realities Discord at https://discord.gg/Y4jHMCU4sv. Use the #dev or #developers channel (whatever the equivalent is).

## Message (keep it casual — Discord isn't Reddit)

Hey folks 👋

I've been building apps for the G2 for a while now and just finished open-sourcing four of them plus a community-maintained developer guide. Posting in case it's useful for anyone else building on the Even Hub:

📖 **Dev guide**: https://github.com/aleapc/even-hub-devguide
Consolidates 24 SDK quirks I hit (including some that only show up on real hardware, not the simulator) and working patterns for things like pixel art, lifecycle events, mobile companion apps for reminders, and distributed backends.

📱 **The four apps** (all MIT licensed):
- **eyefit-g2** — eye exercise coach with pixel art character + IMU head tracking
- **hunter-g2** — place discovery with Overpass/OSM, pixel art category icons, OSRM walking routes
- **speechcoach-g2** — live speech pace coach with real Whisper STT via a Node backend
- **breakmate-g2** — animated pixel art character for health reminders

The guide credits everyone in this community whose repos I learned from — @BxNxM for even-dev, nickustinov's notes, fabioglimb's even-toolkit (where the pixel art upng-js pattern comes from), kqb's openclaw HUD, sam's claude-code-g2, and i-soxi's protocol reverse-engineering. Let me know if I missed you.

Would love PRs, issues, and quirks-I-missed from anyone else shipping on the Hub. I want this to grow into a community reference rather than a personal thing.

Also happy to just answer questions if anyone's hitting weird SDK behavior and wants a second pair of eyes 🙂

## Tips for Discord specifically

- **Post once, don't spam** — Discord doesn't reward repetition the way Reddit/HN does
- **Pin in replies, not multiple posts** — if people reply with questions, answer in-thread
- **Use the appropriate channel** — likely #dev-talk, #projects, or #developers (check the server rules first)
- **Emoji react to others first** — be a community member, not just a drive-by promoter
- **Link to specific sections** — "the pixel art recipe here: [link]" gets more clicks than "check out the whole guide"
